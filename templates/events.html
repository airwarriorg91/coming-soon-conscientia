IEEE Space Startup Conference

Expand the horizons of your technical innovation. IEEE EdSoc at IIST is organising a conference of space startups on 8th and 9th October. The event includes conferences, Q&A sessions, exhibitions to interact and technical workshops. Learn from and network with the new generation of space startups working with the dawn of privatised space technology in India. For students, there is a lot to learn and perhaps an opportunity to join one of these startups on their journey. In conjunction with IEEE IIST EdSoc Student Branch Chapter Inauguration.
